A Journey Beyond Codes and Algorithms

Hello and welcome! I'm a passionate technologist, born in the year 2006, who thrives at the intersection of computer science, innovation, and problem-solving. My journey started in the bustling cityscape of Dubai, where I fell in love with the immense possibilities that technology could offer to enhance our lives and solve real-world problems.

The Genesis: Crafting Computers

At the young age of 12, while most kids were engrossed in video games or sports, I found myself irresistibly drawn to the world of computer hardware. I began building PCs from scratch, investing meticulous care into each component, fully embracing the challenges and triumphs that came with it. This experience served as my introduction to the logical and physical dimensions of technology, allowing me to appreciate the power and potential of well-engineered machines.

Ethical Hacking

My early experiences with hardware naturally segued into an intense curiosity about software and the intricacies of the internet. I ventured into ethical hacking and phishing exercises to understand the vulnerabilities that threaten our digital world. Far from being a rogue endeavor, this phase instilled in me an ethic of responsibility, caution, and most importantly, the knowledge to protect and secure information.

Blockchain and Crypto Maze

The financial revolution led by cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology caught my attention as my next significant milestone. I dived deep into building bots aimed at predicting crypto market trends. My endeavors weren't just about financial gain but were driven by a fascination for data patterns, probability, and the underlying algorithms that could simulate human intuition.

Skillsets that Transcend Boundaries

I equipped myself with a diverse tech stack to actualize my ideas into real-world applications. I mastered Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Swift, acquiring a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end development. The journey didn't stop at self-learning; I went on to validate my skills by earning five prestigious certificates from FreeCodeCamp, a testament to my commitment and proficiency.

The IBDP Chapter

Parallel to my technological pursuits, I successfully completed the rigorous International Baccalaureate Diploma Program (IBDP), which not only honed my academic abilities but also instilled in me a holistic worldview. This curriculum encouraged me to integrate ethical, social, and global contexts in all my endeavors, shaping me into a well-rounded individual.

My Latest Offering
